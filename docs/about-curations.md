## Why We Exist

### The Problem We See

AI is being sold as:
- A replacement for human work
- A way to cut costs
- A tool for extraction and manipulation
- Something to fear or hype

**We reject all of this.**

### What We Believe Instead

**AI is a creative medium.**

Just like photography didn't replace painting‚Äîit gave us a new way to see.
Just like synthesizers didn't replace musicians‚Äîthey gave us new sounds.

**AI doesn't replace human creativity. It gives us new ways to create.**

When used ethically, transparently, and with intention, AI can:
- Amplify underrepresented voices
- Make complex work accessible
- Free humans from repetitive tasks to focus on meaning
- Create things neither human nor AI could build alone

**This is what CURATIONS is for.**

## How We Work

### Our Process

1. **Listen First**
We don't start with AI. We start with: *What are you actually trying to create? Why does it matter?*

2. **Design With Purpose**
Every system, every framework, every line of code asks: *Does this serve the greater good?*

3. **Build Transparently**
We document as we go. Our frameworks, prompts, and systems are open-source.

4. **Collaborate as Partners**
We work WITH you, not FOR you. Your vision + our capability = something neither could build alone.

5. **Share What We Learn**
Everything we discover goes back into the commons. Knowledge should be accessible.

## Our Values in Action

Every project we take asks these questions:

1. **Does this make the world better?**
   If we can't answer yes, we don't build it.

2. **Does this respect human dignity?**
   AI should empower people, not manipulate them.

3. **Is this transparent?**
   No black boxes. No hidden agendas. Full clarity.

4. **Can we share what we learn?**
   If we can't open-source the frameworks, why are we building them?

5. **Will this age well?**
   Not chasing hype. Building things that matter long-term.
