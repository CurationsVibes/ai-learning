The Problem

Current State of AI in K-12 Education

What's happening:

  • Students using ChatGPT to complete assignments (academic integrity crisis)
  • Teachers uncertain about how to incorporate AI responsibly
  • Parents concerned about black-box algorithms and student data privacy
  • School districts want AI literacy programs but lack transparent frameworks

What's Missing

Transparency

How is AI actually being used in student work?

Ethics

Who reviews AI outputs for appropriateness?

Accountability

Can parents see what their children are creating?

Real-World Skills

Are students learning collaboration or just prompt engineering?

The Question: What if AI education was transparent by default?

The CURATIONS Solution

Youth Curator Program: Brand-Funded AI Mentorships with Full Transparency

How It Works:

  • High school juniors/seniors (ages 16-18) work on real brand campaigns
  • Students learn Human × AI collaboration using professional tools
  • Brands fund the work (not schools, not parents)
  • Full transparency for parents, teachers, and school districts

What Makes It Different

  • Transparency Portal – Parents and schools see every AI interaction in real-time
  • Artifact Logs – Every student project tracked with human/AI attribution metadata
  • Ethical Guardrails – AI Curators review all outputs for appropriateness, accuracy, and safety
  • Professional Portfolios – Students graduate with real client work, not just classroom assignments

Technology Stack: Claude, AutoRAG, Vectorize + Google Workspace

Why Google Infrastructure?

School districts already trust Google. We layer transparency on top.

Core Integrations

🔐 Google Single Sign-On (SSO)

Students sign in with school accounts. No new passwords, no data silos. FERPA/COPPA compliant by design.

📚 Google Classroom API

Auto-sync student rosters. Youth Curator assignments appear in teachers' dashboards. Grades sync to Classroom gradebook.

📁 Google Drive API

Parents access student work in real-time via shared folders. Version history shows human edits vs. AI suggestions.

📝 Docs, Sheets, Slides APIs

Students create campaigns using familiar tools. Artifacts logged to Transparency Portal with metadata.

Why This Matters: CURATIONS becomes a showcase for what's possible with Google for Education—driving awareness of advanced API features school districts may not be using yet.

Digital Stewardship & Ethics

Core Principles

Transparent Collaboration

AI as partner, not black box. Every AI interaction logged with timestamp, tool used, output generated. Human decision-making always visible in audit trail.

Privacy First

No personal data stored in AI consultations. FERPA/COPPA compliance built into system architecture. Students control what's shared with portfolio vs. kept private.

Parent Visibility

Real-time access to student work via Google Drive integration. Transparency Portal dashboard shows AI usage metrics per project. Opt-in notifications for major milestones.

Responsible Innovation

Ethical AI guardrails: Molly Towns (AI Curator) reviews all outputs. Human mentors provide strategic oversight. Students learn to question AI suggestions.

First in K-12: Full transparency for parents, schools, and districts—setting the standard for ethical AI education.
