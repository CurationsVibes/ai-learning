## ‚ö° Quick Wins

Before diving into the full curriculum, here are immediate upgrades:

### 1. **The "Let's think step by step" Upgrade**
Add "Let's think through this step by step" to any complex question. This simple phrase activates chain-of-thought reasoning.

### 2. **The Role Assignment**
Start prompts with "You are an expert [profession] with 20 years of experience..." to unlock domain-specific knowledge.

### 3. **The Output Format**
End prompts with "Format your response as..." to get structured, usable outputs every time.

### 4. **The Iterative Refinement**
Never accept the first response. Ask "What's missing?" or "How could this be improved?" to trigger self-critique.
