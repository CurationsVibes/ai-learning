## üõ†Ô∏è Tips for Better Conversations

### DO's ‚úÖ

1. **Be specific** - "Help me write a cover letter for a marketing job" beats "Help me write"
2. **Provide context** - Share relevant background information
3. **Ask follow-up questions** - Dig deeper when you need more info
4. **Experiment** - Try different ways of asking
5. **Use examples** - "Like [example]" helps AI understand what you want
6. **Break down complex requests** - Ask step-by-step rather than all at once

### DON'Ts ‚ùå

1. **Don't overshare personal info** - No passwords, SSNs, financial details
2. **Don't assume perfection** - AI can make mistakes; verify important facts
3. **Don't give up too quickly** - Rephrase if you don't get what you need
4. **Don't be vague** - "Help me" is too broad; be specific
5. **Don't treat it like a search engine** - It's conversational, not just keywords

## üí° Understanding AI Responses

### What to Expect

**AI will typically**:
- Respond in complete sentences
- Ask clarifying questions if needed
- Provide structured, organized information
- Admit when it's unsure or doesn't know something
- Offer to help further or in different ways

**AI might**:
- Make occasional mistakes (always verify important info)
- Provide generic answers to vague questions
- Ask for more context to give better help
- Offer multiple perspectives or approaches

## üîÑ The Conversation Flow

### Natural Progression

1. **Start broad** - Introduce your topic or need
2. **Get specific** - Ask focused follow-up questions
3. **Clarify** - Request examples or more detail
4. **Apply** - Ask how to use what you've learned
5. **Wrap up** - Summarize or ask for final tips
