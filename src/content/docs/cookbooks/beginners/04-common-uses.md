## üí° Tips for Each Use Case

### For Better Results:

1. **Be specific** about what you need
2. **Provide context** about your situation
3. **Specify format** (bullet points, essay, list, etc.)
4. **State constraints** (length, budget, time, etc.)
5. **Iterate** if the first response isn't quite right

### Remember:

- **Always verify facts** - especially for important decisions
- **Don't share sensitive info** - no passwords, personal data
- **Use AI as a starting point** - add your own expertise and judgment
- **Combine AI help with human insight** - you're the expert on your needs
