## Quick Start Tips

Before diving into the full guide, here are some immediate tips:

1. **Start Simple** - Don't try to do everything at once. Start with simple questions.
2. **Be Specific** - The more specific your question, the better the answer you'll get.
3. **Experiment** - Try different ways of asking the same question.
4. **Learn Iteratively** - Each conversation teaches you something about how AI works.
