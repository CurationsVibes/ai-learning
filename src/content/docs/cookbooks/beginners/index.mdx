## ‚ö° Quick Start Tips

Before diving into the full guide, here are immediate wins:

### 1. **Start Simple**
Don't try complex tasks first. Begin with simple questions like "Explain [topic] to me like I'm new to this."

### 2. **Be Specific**
The more specific your question, the better the answer. "Help me write an email to my manager about taking Friday off" beats "Help me write."

### 3. **Experiment Freely**
AI won't judge you for "bad" questions. Try different approaches. Ask the same thing three ways. Learn what works.

### 4. **Verify Important Facts**
AI can be confidently wrong. For anything important (health, legal, financial), always verify with authoritative sources.

### 5. **Iterate and Refine**
Your first response isn't final. Ask "Can you improve this?" or "Make it shorter" or "What's missing?"

## ‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

<details>
<summary><strong>Which AI should I use ‚Äî ChatGPT, Claude, or Gemini?</strong></summary>

They're all excellent for beginners! **ChatGPT** is the most popular and widely accessible. **Claude** excels at nuanced, thoughtful responses. **Gemini** integrates well with Google services. Start with whichever is easiest to access ‚Äî the core skills transfer between all platforms.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Is AI safe to use?</strong></summary>

Yes, when used responsibly. Don't share sensitive personal information (social security numbers, passwords, private health details). Remember AI can make mistakes ‚Äî verify important information. Be aware of potential biases. Our Safety & Ethics module covers this in detail.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How much does AI cost?</strong></summary>

Many AI assistants offer free tiers! ChatGPT, Claude, and Gemini all have free versions that are perfect for beginners. Paid tiers ($20/month typically) offer faster responses and access to more capable models, but free versions are more than sufficient for learning.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>Can AI replace my job?</strong></summary>

AI is best viewed as a productivity multiplier, not a replacement. People who learn to work effectively with AI will likely be more valuable, not less. This guide teaches you to harness AI as a powerful tool for whatever you do.

</details>

<details>
<summary><strong>How long until I'm "good" at using AI?</strong></summary>

You can have productive AI conversations within minutes of starting. Basic proficiency comes within a few days of regular use. This course (about 75 minutes) covers the fundamentals. True mastery is ongoing ‚Äî you'll keep discovering new capabilities and techniques.

</details>
