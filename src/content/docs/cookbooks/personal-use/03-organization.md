## üîß Customization Tips

1. **Start small** - Don't try to implement everything at once
2. **Match your energy** - Schedule hard tasks for peak hours
3. **Build in flexibility** - Life happens, plans adjust
4. **Review regularly** - What works changes over time
5. **Celebrate progress** - Track wins, not just tasks
